CC90HFE

CAUTION: Don't use CC90 version 2.0 with programs that require the 1.03 or earlier versions of CC90: they are not compatible.

CC90HFE allows you to create true images of your Thomson double density floppies into the HFE format, which is supported by Teo and is the format used by the HxC Floppy Emulator.

You can send us by email your HFE disks created with CC90HFE. They will be published on the software page of LogicielsMoto.
